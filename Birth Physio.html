SERVICES AT BIRTH PHYSIO

Childbirth Education Classes

Prepare with knowledge. Birth with confidence.

These sessions help you understand what happens during labour and birth, so you can make informed choices and feel emotionally prepared.

What we cover:

  • Understanding labour & stages of birth
  • Pain coping strategies (breathing, movement, relaxation)
  • Labour positions & partner support
  • Birth planning & informed decision-making
  • Preparing mentally and physically for birth

Antenatal Physiotherapy

Move comfortably. Prepare your body for birth.

  • Back, pelvic & hip pain management
  • Pelvic floor awareness & strengthening
  • Core stability & posture correction
  • Breathing & mobility for labour preparation

Best suited for:

  • Any trimester: Pain, discomfort, or preventive care

Postnatal Physiotherapy

Recover safely. Rebuild strength. Feel confident again.

  • Core & pelvic floor rehabilitation
  • Diastasis recti management
  • Postnatal pain & posture correction
  • Safe return to daily activities & exercise

Best suited for:

  • From 6 weeks postpartum onwards
  • New mothers at any recovery stage
